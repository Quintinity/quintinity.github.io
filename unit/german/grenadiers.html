Grenadiers

The Grenadier is the core infantry soldier of the German army. These troops are trained and experienced, capably led, and well equipped--the equal of any soldier in the war.

Effective against infantry at long range. Versatile abilities.


Veterancy

480 XP

Unlocks the 'Field First Aid' veteran ability.

960 XP

+40% accuracy.

1920 XP

-20% weapon cooldown, -25% Panzerfaust recharge, -23% received accuracy.



Upgrades

MG42 LMG

The MG-42 is one of the best machine guns in the war, offering an unparalleled rate of fire.

Improved anti-infantry capability. Does not fire on the move.

Upgrade Time: 30s

60

Jaeger Infantry Package

Two semi-automatic G43 rifles add increased firepower. The squad can cross into enemy areas undetected and interrogate enemy casualties to reveal their comrades' positions.

Upgrade available on Panzergrenadiers, Grenadiers, and Stormtroopers.

Upgrade Time: 30s

45

Ambush Camouflage

Infantry in cover can camouflage themselves from enemies. Ambushing enemy units while camouflaged will briefly increase the squads damage.

Allows squad to camouflage in cover. Brief increase in accuracy when attacking from stealth.

Upgrade Time: 30s

20

Veteran Squad Leader

Command releases a veteran squad leader to increases the effectiveness of the unit.

Grants a squad leader armed with a G43. Increases squad survivability, rate of fire and removes the cost of the 'Field First Aid' ability.

Upgrade Time: 30s

60


Abilities

Hold Fire

Hold fire until explicitly ordered to attack.

Toggle ability.

Hull Down

Pioneers, Grenadiers and Panzer Grenadiers are able to set German armor in an immobile Hull Down position, increasing defenses and damage.

Left-click ability, left-click target vehicle.

Field First Aid

The squad has emergency medical supplies that allow the treatment of other injured soldiers.

Left-click ability, left-click target squad.

15

Rally at Forward Supply Station
