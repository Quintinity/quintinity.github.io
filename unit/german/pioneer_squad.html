Pioneer Squad

Pioneers are basic combat troops whose primary tasks are building structures, creating or clearing battlefield obstacles, and attacking enemy defensive positions.

Can be upgraded to clear mines and cut wire, or equipped with a flamethrower.


Veterancy

400 XP

Unlocks the 'Field First Aid' veteran ability.

800 XP

+20% accuracy, +1 HP/s repair rate per model.

1600 XP

No longer takes extra damage while repairing, +15% accuracy, -15% weapon cooldown.

Abilities

Repair

Demonstrating pure ingenuity, troops can repair any damaged structure, vehicle, or bridge with a repair rate of 1.6 HP/s per model. Conscripts repair with a rate of 1.06 HP/s per model.

Right-click on damaged object, or select Repair and left-click.

Hull Down

Pioneers, Grenadiers and Panzer Grenadiers are able to set German armor in an immobile Hull Down position, increasing defenses and damage.

Left-click ability, left-click target vehicle.

Field First Aid

The squad has emergency medical supplies that allow the treatment of other injured soldiers.

Left-click ability, left-click target squad.

15

Rally at Forward Supply Station

Unit is uncontrollable while rallying.

Sprint

The unit will move very quickly for a short period of time. Weapons cannot be fired while moving.
