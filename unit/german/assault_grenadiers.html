Assault Grenadiers

Assault Grenadiers are specially equipped with MP40s and model 24 grenades to clear enemy units out of defensive positions.

Effective against infantry at close range.


Veterancy

560 XP

-10% received accuracy, -25% sprint recharge.

1120 XP

-25% weapon cooldown, -29% received accuracy.

2240 XP

+40% accuracy, +25% grenade range.



Abilities

Casualty Interrogation

Interrogate enemy casualties to briefly reveal all enemy positions on the mini-map.

Left-click ability, left-click enemy casualty.

Hold Fire

Hold fire until explicitly ordered to attack.

Toggle ability.

Hull Down

Pioneers, Grenadiers and Panzer Grenadiers are able to set German armor in an immobile Hull Down position, increasing defenses and damage.

Left-click ability, left-click target vehicle.

Model 24 Stielgranate Grenade Assault

The Model 24 "Potato Masher" grenade is the standard German anti infantry grenade, used to clear enemies out of entrenched positions. Every member of the squad will throw grenades.

Left-click ability, left-click target. Large blast radius.
