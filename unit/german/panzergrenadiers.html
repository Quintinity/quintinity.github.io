Panzergrenadiers

Panzergrenadiers are well-trained infantry who can fight infantry or vehicles when properly equipped. These elite infantry carry assault rifles and grenades.

Effective at medium range vs infantry. Can be upgraded with anti-tank weapons.


Veterancy

680 XP

Unlocks the 'Field First Aid' veteran ability, -16% received accuracy.

1360 XP

-25% weapon cooldown, -16% received accuracy.

2720 XP

+40% accuracy, +25% grenade range.

Abilities

Hold Fire

Hold fire until explicitly ordered to attack.

Toggle ability.

Hull Down

Pioneers, Grenadiers and Panzer Grenadiers are able to set German armor in an immobile Hull Down position, increasing defenses and damage.

Left-click ability, left-click target vehicle.

Field First Aid

The squad has emergency medical supplies that allow the treatment of other injured soldiers.

Left-click ability, left-click target squad.

15

Rally at Forward Supply Station

The unit will move quickly to the nearest forward supply station.

Unit is uncontrollable while rallying.

Sprint

The unit will move very quickly for a short period of time. Weapons cannot be fired while moving.

Duration 5s.

10

Bundled Model 24 Grenades

Throws a baled load of seven Model 24 grenades at the target location.

Left-click ability, left-click target area. Effective vs infantry, light vehicles.

35

(PASSIVE) Combined Arms

Trained to operate and work in tandem with nearby vehicles, Panzergrenadiers will move more quickly and be more difficult to hit.

Increases speed and reduces received accuracy when near vehicles.

Rudimentary Repair

Basic training allows Panzergrenadiers to conduct field repairs on vehicles and structures.

Right-click on damaged object, or select Repair and left-click.

Mark Target

Panzergrenadiers will mark a hostile infantry unit, making them more susceptible to incoming fire. The Panzergrenadiers will be more vulnerable to incoming fire during this ability's duration.

Left-click ability, left-click target infantry squad. 15 second duration.

25

Model 24 Stun Grenade

Model 24 Stun Grenades will do light damage and temporarily shock infantry units in an area.

Left-click ability, left-click target position. Stuns infantry for 5 seconds, reducing weapon range and movement.
