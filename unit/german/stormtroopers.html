Stormtroopers

Elite infiltration troops who are experts at disrupting enemy supply lines and ambush tactics. These units can be deployed from any ambient building.

Strong at long range vs infantry and ideal for harassing enemy supply lines and flanking attacks.

Abilities

Hull Down

Pioneers, Grenadiers and Panzer Grenadiers are able to set German armor in an immobile Hull Down position, increasing defenses and damage.

Left-click ability, left-click target vehicle.

Sprint

The unit will move very quickly for a short period of time. Weapons cannot be fired while moving.

Duration 5s.

10

Rally at Forward Supply Station

The unit will move quickly to the nearest forward supply station.

Unit is uncontrollable while rallying.

Tactical Advance

By steadying their weapons and focusing their fire, Stormtroopers attack more effectively, but are consequently more vulnerable to enemy attacks.

Click to activate. Duration 10s. Modifiers: -2 posture speed, +50% received accuracy, +50% weapon accuracy. While moving: +150% weapon burst length.

25

Vehicle Detection

Stormtroopers are able to detect enemy vehicles through recognition of their specific noise signatures.

Duration 30s. Enemy vehicles appear on the mini-map.

30

Casualty Interrogation

Left-click ability, left-click enemy casualty.
