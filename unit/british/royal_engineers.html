Royal Engineers

British Royal Engineers are basic close combat troops whose primary tasks are building and destroying structures and fortifications.

Effective against infantry at close range.

Heavy Engineers

The Royal Engineers get additional equipment, allowing them to construct buildings and repair faster but limiting their mobility in combat. They also are equipped with a Vickers K Light Machine Gun.

Royal Engineers repair and construct faster. The Vickers K LMG is effective against infantry. Modifiers: +25% armor, +100% construction rate, +0.525 HP/s repair rate per model. While in combat: -25% movement speed.

Abilities

Specialist Repair

Royal Engineers can repair any damaged structure, vehicle, or bridge with a repair rate of 1.6 HP/s per model. If the target is a British vehicle, the engineers will enable Emergency War Speed on the vehicle.

Right-click on damaged object, or select Repair and left-click.
