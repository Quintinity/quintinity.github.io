Valentine Mk. XI Tank

The Valentine Mk. XI Tank is a light infantry tank, improved over earlier variants with more powerful engine and a long barrel 75mm main gun.

Effective against infantry and light armor.


Veterancy

1020 XP

Unlocks the 'Concentrated Sexton Barrage' veteran ability, +7 sight range.

2040 XP

+20% maximum speed, +20% rotation speed, +35% turret rotation speed.

4080 XP

+25% health, -10% received accuracy.



Abilities

Emergency War Speed

Increases maximum speed by pushing the vehicle's engine beyond its rated power for a short duration. Royal Engineers have made mechanical improvements to allow for this.

Left-click to activate. Duration 15s. Can only be used once until unlocked again. Modifiers: +15% maximum speed, +30% acceleration. While moving: -25% received accuracy.

Concentrated Sexton Barrage

Orders all available Sextons to fire long range supercharged rounds at the target area.

Left-click ability, left-click target location. Each Sexton will fire 8 shells at the designated target.

35

Observation Mode

The Tank Commander and Crew will cease fire and survey the area for hostiles.

Toggle Ability. Increases sight. Disables main gun.

Smoke Screen

The Valentine's smoke grenade projector launches six shells at the target area in quick succession.

Left-click ability, left-click target area.

Prioritize Vehicles
