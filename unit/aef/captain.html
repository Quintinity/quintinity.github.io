Captain

Gains veterancy from nearby allies. Effective at medium range vs. infantry.

Abilities

On Me!

Nearby infantry, even if suppressed, will rally on the Captain.

Left-click ability, left-click target infantry squad. Modifiers: Remove suppression, +1 posture speed & suppression immunity for 4 seconds.

Supervise

Captain can Supervise any own Command Post structure, decreasing the production time of any ordered unit.

Left-click to activate, left-click to target Command Post. Gaining Veterancy will yield improved production rates

Halt Retreat

The officer will halt the retreat of squads under his command.

M23 Smoke Screen Grenade

Fires a rifle grenade smoke round that creates a line of smoke in the targeted direction. Any vehicles hit by the grenade will be temporarily blinded and slowed.
