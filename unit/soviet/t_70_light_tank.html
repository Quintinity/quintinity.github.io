T-70 Light Tank

The T-70 Light Tank is mobile and has good armor for a light tank. The 45mm gun resides in a one-man turret; commander has to choose targets, fire guns, and direct the driver.

Effective against infantry and light vehicles. Can enter Recon Mode.
