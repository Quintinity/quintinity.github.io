M5 Half-track Transport

The M5 Half-track is a lend-lease provision to the Soviet Union. The open-topped M5 is spacious enough to carry two squads, and its .50cal machine gun provides good firepower.

Transports infantry. Transported troops can fire out of the M5. Light armor is easily penetrated.
