Commissar Command Squad

The Soviet Commissar squad is a potent command unit that inspires nearby soviet infantry improving their combat effectiveness.

Effective at medium range against infantry. Can heal and increase the effectiveness of nearby infantry.


Veterancy

580 XP

Increases squad size by 1.

1060 XP

+40% weapon accuracy, +5 targeted ability range.

2120 XP

-29% weapon cooldown, -23% received accuracy.



Abilities

Hold the Line

The Commissar motivates nearby soldiers to hold their ground at any cost.

Left-click ability, left-click friendly squad. Increases target squad's defenses, but greatly reduces movement speed. Bonuses are not active during retreat.

Not One Step Back

The Commissar instructs the troops to fight to the last to push the enemy back.

Left-click ability, left-click friendly squad. Selected unit has increased accuracy and rate of fire, but is easier to hit.

Fear Propaganda Artillery

The Commissar calls in a barrage of propaganda to demoralize the enemy.

Left-click ability, left-click target enemy squad.

45

Distribute Medical Supplies

The Commissar squad will heal themselves and nearby friendly troops, if they are out of combat.

Click to activate. Duration 30s.
