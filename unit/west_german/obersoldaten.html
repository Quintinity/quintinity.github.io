Infiltration Grenade Assault

The Model 24 "Potato Masher" grenade is the standard German anti infantry grenade. Every member of the squad will throw a grenade.

Left-click ability, left-click target area. Effective vs infantry, light vehicles.

15

Suppressing Fire

Accurate fire from the Obersoldaten squad will force hostile squads to ground.

Left-click ability, left-click target enemy infantry squad. Reduces damage temporarily in exchange for suppression.

Blendkörper 2H Frangible Smoke Grenade

The Blendkörper 2H Frangible Smoke Grenade uses caustic titanium tetrachloride to create a smoke screen and harm nearby infantry through acute exposure. Obscures line of sight and disables vehicle weapons if they stay within it.

Left-click ability, left-click target. Smoke lasts for about 20s.

30

Bundled Grenade

Throws a baled load of seven Model 24 grenades at the target location.

Left-click ability, left-click target area. Effective vs infantry, light vehicles.
