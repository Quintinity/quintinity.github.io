Sturmpioneer Squad

Sturmpioneers are tough combat troops whose primary tasks are creating or clearing battlefield obstacles and attacking enemy defensive positions.

Can be upgraded to clear mines and cut wire. No movement penalties when moving through rough terrain.

Abilities

Repair

Highly skilled, Sturmpioneers can repair any damaged structure, vehicle, or bridge with a repair rate of 2 HP/s per model.

Right-click on damaged object, or select Repair and left-click.

Salvage Operations

Use on wrecked vehicles / team weapons for 5 Fuel, abandoned vehicles for 12 Fuel.

Left-click ability, left-click target.

Concussive Grenade

Infantry hit by the grenade will be staggered and slowed down for a period of time.

Left-click ability, left-click target area. Effective vs. infantry.

30

Medical Supplies

Three boxes of Medical Supplies are dropped for infantry to pick up.

Left-click ability, left-click target position. Nearby infantry are healed when they pick up medical supplies.

45

Thorough Salvage

Use on wrecked vehicles / team weapons for 5 Fuel and 15 Munitions, abandoned vehicles for 12 Fuel and 36 Munitions.

Click and target vehicle or team weapon.

Activate Minesweeper

The minesweeper allows mines to be detected and defused, but the soldier holding the minesweeper cannot fire their weapon. The minesweeper can be put away to give the squad back their full combat potential.

Toggle ability. Can detect mines while active.

Put Away Minesweeper

The minesweeper allows mines to be detected and defused, but the soldier holding the minesweeper cannot fire their weapon. The minesweeper can be put away to give the squad back their full combat potential.

Toggle ability. Can detect mines while active.

Wire Cutters

Wire Cutters are used to cut through barbed wire fences or barbed wire fields.

Right-click barbed wire or Left-click Wire Cutters and target enemy barbed wire.
