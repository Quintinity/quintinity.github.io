sWS Supply Half-track

The sWS Supply Half-track (schwerer Wehrmachtsschlepper) can be positioned on any captured territory that is in supply.

Must be positioned on any owned territory that is in supply.



Abilities

Convert to Battlegroup Headquarters

Permanently convert the sWS Supply Half-track to the Battlegroup Headquarters to order in units that specialize in ranged reconnaissance and attacks.

Retreat point, available medics can heal nearby infantry.

Convert to Schwerer Panzer Headquarters

Permanently convert the sWS Supply Half-track to the Schwerer Panzer Headquarters. Potent infantry and medium tanks.

Armed with powerful 3.7cm Flak cannon.

Convert to Mechanized Regiment Headquarters

Permanently convert the sWS Supply Half-track to the Mechanized Regiment Headquarters to order in versatile light vehicles for anti-infantry and anti-vehicle combat.

Available repair pioneers can repair nearby damaged vehicles.

Emergency Repairs

Vehicle crews gain the ability to repair critical damage on their vehicles. After 3 seconds upon activating, one critical will be fixed and repairs start at a rate of 30 HP/s for 12 seconds. Prioritizes the 'Heavy Engine Damage' critical.

Duration 15s. Vehicle is immobile and weapons are inactive.
