Sturm Offizier

This Officer and his squad inspire nearby Oberkommando units to fight harder and has a number of abilities to weaken enemy units. If the Sturm Offizier is killed, nearby units will retreat.

This Officer is escorted by his Obersoldaten bodyguards, effective at range.



Abilities

Target Them!

Units will focus their fire on the specified target, shooting to kill. Enemy units affected by this ability are easier to hit.

Left-click ability, left-click target infantry squad.

40

Force Retreat

A combination of fear and fire forces an enemy unit to fall back. The sight of their comrades being forced back inspires other nearby enemy units to fight harder.

Left-click ability, left-click target enemy squad.

60
