Mobile Assault Regiment

This Regiment provides numerous opportunities to carry out aggressive plays. Utilize flamethrowers on the Royal Engineers to clear out garrisoned positions. Create advantageous scenarios by attacking behind enemy lines with Commandos. Advanced Cover Combat will allow your infantry sections to make aggressive attacks in open areas of the map. The Land Mattress rocket artillery will help you dislodge entrenched positions. Activate Vehicle Crew Repairs to reduce the time it takes to repair damaged vehicles.
