Luftwaffe Supply Doctrine

The Luftwaffe Supply doctrine utilizes the German Air Force in a support role for the ground troops. Ju52 cargo planes can deliver valuable resources and medical supplies to troops on the ground, while Stuka bombers will provide reconnaissance and destructive power against static positions.
