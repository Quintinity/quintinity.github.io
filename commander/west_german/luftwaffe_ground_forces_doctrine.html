Luftwaffe Ground Forces Doctrine

Supporting forces of the Luftwaffe will fight on the ground alongside the army. Recon enemy positions and set up heavy fortifications and flak emplacements while using the elite Fallschirmjäger to flank and destroy. Use an airborne assault to hold key enemy territories.
